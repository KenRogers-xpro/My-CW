Admissions

Admission Process

Thank you for your interest in Bishop Nankyama Memorial College. We welcome applications from students who are committed to academic excellence and personal growth.

1

Application

Complete and submit the application form with all required documents.

2

Entrance Exam

Take the entrance examination to assess academic readiness.

3

Interview

Attend an interview with the school administration.

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Recent passport-sized photographs (4)
  • Previous school reports (last 2 years)
  • PLE results slip (for O Level applicants)
  • UCE certificate (for A Level applicants)
  • Recommendation letter from previous school

Fees Structure

Our fees are structured to provide quality education while remaining affordable. The fees cover tuition, boarding (for boarding students), meals, and basic medical care.

O Level Fees

Day Students: UGX 800,000 per term
Boarding Students: UGX 1,200,000 per term
Development Fee (one-time): UGX 300,000

A Level Fees

Day Students: UGX 900,000 per term
Boarding Students: UGX 1,350,000 per term
Development Fee (one-time): UGX 300,000

Scholarships

Bishop Nankyama Memorial College offers a limited number of scholarships to academically gifted students from disadvantaged backgrounds. The scholarships cover partial or full tuition fees depending on the student's circumstances and academic performance.

To apply for a scholarship, please complete the scholarship application form and submit it along with your admission application.
