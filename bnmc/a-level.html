A Level Program

Program Overview

Our A Level program prepares students for the Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) examinations. The program spans two years (Senior 5 and Senior 6) and offers specialized subject combinations to align with students' career aspirations.

At Bishop Nankyama Memorial College, we provide a conducive learning environment that encourages academic excellence, critical thinking, and independent research. Our A Level program is designed to prepare students for higher education and future careers.

Subject Combinations

We offer the following subject combinations:

Science Combinations

  • PCM: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics
    Ideal for Engineering, Architecture, Computer Science
  • PCB: Physics, Chemistry, Biology
    Ideal for Medicine, Dentistry, Pharmacy
  • BCM: Biology, Chemistry, Mathematics
    Ideal for Biomedical Sciences, Veterinary Medicine

Arts Combinations

  • HEG: History, Economics, Geography
    Ideal for Law, International Relations, Social Sciences
  • DEL: Divinity, Economics, Literature
    Ideal for Journalism, Education, Religious Studies
  • HEL: History, Economics, Literature
    Ideal for Law, Journalism, Public Administration

Technical/Vocational Combinations

  • AEM: Agriculture, Entrepreneurship, Mathematics
    Ideal for Agricultural Sciences, Agribusiness
  • CEM: Computer Science, Economics, Mathematics
    Ideal for Information Technology, Business Computing

All students are required to take General Paper (GP) and Computer Studies as subsidiary subjects.

Entry Requirements

To join our A Level program, students must meet the following requirements:

  • Minimum of 8 passes in UCE (O Level) with at least 5 credits in relevant subjects
  • For Science combinations: Credits in Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry, and/or Biology
  • For Arts combinations: Credits in English, History, Geography, and/or Religious Education
  • Satisfactory performance in the school's entrance examination
  • Successful interview with the school administration

Assessment and Examinations

Students are assessed through:

  • Continuous assessment tests and assignments
  • Mid-term examinations
  • End-of-term examinations
  • Mock examinations in Senior 6
  • Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) UACE examinations at the end of Senior 6

Why Choose Our A Level Program?

  • Specialized Teaching: Subject specialists with extensive experience in A Level curriculum.
  • Advanced Facilities: Well-equipped science laboratories, computer lab, and library with A Level resources.
  • University Preparation: Career guidance and university application support.
  • Research Opportunities: Students are encouraged to undertake research projects in their areas of interest.
  • Excellent Results: Consistently high performance in UACE examinations with many students qualifying for government scholarships.
