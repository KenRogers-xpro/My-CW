About Bishop Nankyama Memorial College

Our History

Bishop Nankyama Memorial College was founded in 1985 by a group of dedicated educators led by the late Bishop James Nankyama, who had a vision to establish a center of academic excellence that would nurture young minds and prepare them for leadership roles in society.

The school began with just two classrooms and 50 students. Over the years, it has grown into a prestigious institution with modern facilities, a diverse student body, and a reputation for academic excellence. Today, Bishop Nankyama Memorial College stands as a testament to the founder's vision and dedication to quality education.

Our Mission

To provide holistic education that nurtures intellectual, physical, social, and moral development, preparing students to be responsible citizens and leaders in a rapidly changing global society.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in secondary education, recognized nationally and internationally for producing well-rounded graduates who excel academically and contribute positively to society.

Our Core Values

  • Excellence: We strive for the highest standards in all aspects of education.
  • Integrity: We uphold honesty, transparency, and ethical conduct in all our dealings.
  • Respect: We value diversity and treat all individuals with dignity and respect.
  • Responsibility: We encourage accountability and commitment to personal and social responsibilities.
  • Innovation: We embrace creativity and innovative approaches to teaching and learning.
  • Community: We foster a sense of belonging and service to the wider community.
