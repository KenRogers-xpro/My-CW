O Level Program

Program Overview

Our O Level program follows the Uganda National Curriculum and prepares students for the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) examinations. The program spans four years (Senior 1 to Senior 4) and provides a comprehensive foundation in various subjects.

At Bishop Nankyama Memorial College, we emphasize both academic excellence and character development. Our experienced teachers use modern teaching methodologies to ensure that students not only understand the curriculum but also develop critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Subjects Offered

Our O Level curriculum includes a wide range of subjects to provide students with a well-rounded education:

Compulsory Subjects

  • English Language
  • Mathematics
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Geography
  • History
  • Religious Education

Optional Subjects

  • Computer Studies
  • Fine Art
  • Music
  • Agriculture
  • Commerce
  • Entrepreneurship
  • French
  • Literature in English

Students are required to take all compulsory subjects and select a minimum of two optional subjects based on their interests and career aspirations.

Entry Requirements

To join our O Level program, students must meet the following requirements:

  • Successful completion of Primary Leaving Examinations (PLE) with a minimum of Aggregate 20
  • Satisfactory performance in the school's entrance examination
  • Successful interview with the school administration
  • Recommendation letter from previous school

Assessment and Examinations

Students are assessed through:

  • Continuous assessment tests and assignments
  • Mid-term examinations
  • End-of-term examinations
  • Mock examinations in Senior 4
  • Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) UCE examinations at the end of Senior 4

Why Choose Our O Level Program?

  • Experienced Teachers: Our faculty consists of highly qualified and experienced teachers dedicated to student success.
  • Modern Facilities: Well-equipped laboratories, library, and computer lab to enhance the learning experience.
  • Small Class Sizes: We maintain small class sizes to ensure personalized attention for each student.
  • Holistic Development: Focus on academic excellence, character formation, and co-curricular activities.
  • Excellent Track Record: Consistently high performance in UCE examinations.
