About Our School

Bishop Nankyama Memorial College is a prestigious mixed day and boarding secondary school committed to providing quality education and holistic development for all students. Founded in 1985, our institution has a rich history of academic excellence and character formation.

Our mission is to nurture responsible, innovative, and compassionate leaders who will make positive contributions to society through quality education rooted in strong moral values.

Academic Programs

We offer comprehensive O and A level programs designed to prepare students for higher education and future careers.

O Level Program

Our O Level program follows the Uganda National Curriculum and prepares students for the Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE) examinations. Students study a wide range of subjects including Mathematics, English, Sciences, and Humanities.

Learn More

A Level Program

Our A Level program prepares students for the Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE) examinations. Students can choose from various subject combinations in Sciences, Arts, and Technical fields to align with their career aspirations.
