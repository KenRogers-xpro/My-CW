About Ninaz Restaurant

Founded in 2010, Ninaz Restaurant has been serving exquisite cuisine in a warm and elegant atmosphere for over a decade. Our passion for culinary excellence and exceptional service has made us a favorite dining destination.

Chef Nina, our founder, brings her international experience and love for local ingredients to create a menu that celebrates both tradition and innovation. Every dish is crafted with care, using the freshest seasonal ingredients sourced from local farmers and suppliers.

Our mission is to provide an unforgettable dining experience that delights all the senses and creates lasting memories for our guests.
